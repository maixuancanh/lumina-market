## 🎬 Voice Recording Tips

### **Pacing Guide:**
- **Introduction:** Slow, professional (150 words/min)
- **Diagram explanation:** Clear, deliberate
- **Demo sections:** Energetic, confident
- **Conclusion:** Strong, persuasive

### **Emphasis Points:**
- **"Problem"** - emphasize the market gap
- **"Intelligent LP Platform"** - highlight innovation
- **"No-code"** - stress accessibility
- **"24/7 monitoring"** - emphasize automation
- **"Real data"** - stress authenticity
- **"Optimized LP Position"** - highlight outcome

### **Tone Variations:**
- **Problem statement:** Serious, concerned
- **Solution presentation:** Confident, excited
- **Technical demo:** Clear, educational
- **Results showcase:** Proud, accomplished

### **Visual Cues to Mention:**
- "As you can see in our diagram..."
- "The arrow shows the flow from..."
- "In the left lane, you see..."
- "On the right side, we have..."
- "The connecting arrows demonstrate..."

## 🎯 Technical Notes for Voice Artist

### **Pronunciation Guide:**
- **Lumina:** loo-MEE-nah
- **Strategists:** STRAT-uh-jists
- **APY:** A-P-Y (spell out letters)
- **Zap:** ZAP (short, sharp)
- **Solana:** so-LAH-nah
- **DeFi:** DEE-FYE

### **Key Terms to Emphasize:**
- **AI-powered:** stress "AI"
- **No-code:** stress "No"
- **24/7:** stress "24/7"
- **Real-time:** stress "Real"
- **Transparent:** stress "Trans-pa-rent"

### **Pausing Strategy:**
- **Before key terms:** short pause for emphasis
- **After technical explanations:** brief pause for comprehension
- **Between sections:** 2-second pause for transitions
- **Before conclusion:** dramatic pause for impact
